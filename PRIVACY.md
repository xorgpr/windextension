# Privacy Policy — Wind Sports Helper

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Data Collection

This extension **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** to any external server.

- Your rider weight and equipment chart are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
- Wind and gust values are read directly from the forecast pages you visit (windguru.cz, windfinder.com).
- All calculations (equipment size recommendations) are performed entirely within your browser.
- No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, no network requests of any kind.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Read wind forecast data from the active tab on supported websites. |
| `storage` | Save your rider weight and equipment chart locally between sessions. |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.

## Changes

Any changes to this policy will be reflected in the extension's listing and this document.
